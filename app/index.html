Projects

where I worked
2012 - Present

Remote spatial reasoning under uncertainty

How do people communicate spatial information through mobile devices? How can maps and interactions on mobile devices be designed to facilitate spatial inferences and reasoning under uncertainty? How do people utilize spatial cues and landmarks to make spatial inferences? How are spatial cues for indoor and outdoor navigation different? This project aims at understanding and supporting spatial reasoning under uncertainty when spatial knowledge may be distributed across people in remote locations.
